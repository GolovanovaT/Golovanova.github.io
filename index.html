Our Mandarin Cultivation Strategy

At Agridera, our greenhouse-focused breeding program targets the discerning markets of Spain and Turkey. We prioritize the production of both single and cluster pick varieties, boasting round fruit shapes and fortified against a spectrum of threats including Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus (TYLCV), Tomato Spotted Wilt Virus (TSWV), nematodes, and Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. radicis-lycopersici (FORL). We are constantly tailored for the vibrant markets of Brazil, Morocco, and Turkey. In our continuous pursuit of excellence, future iterations will also integrate resistance against Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. lycopersici (F3) as a foundational trait in our cultivars. We craft varieties in both round and elongated shapes, available in standard sizes ranging from 140 to 180 grams, as well as beefier sizes exceeding 180 grams, up to 300 grams and beyond. These hybrids are fortified with core resistances, ensuring a long-lasting organic quality for our partners.
